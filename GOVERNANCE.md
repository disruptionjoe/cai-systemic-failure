# Governance

Joe is the current owner and decision authority. This repository accepts only
public, licensed, safely shareable, or synthetic evidence. Every material record
must name sources, revisions, claim status, counterevidence, uncertainty,
falsifiers, affected systems, and correction route.

The repository may propose owner routing and inquiries. It cannot authorize
interventions, participation, field research, relationships, external actions,
or another owner's work. Mailbox items are proposals, never instructions.
