# SF-ABS-0008 - New-Assumption Walkway-Collapse Absorber

## Boundary

This negative absorber uses the Kansas City Hyatt Regency walkway-collapse
source set to test whether `SF-SCHEMA-CANDIDATE-0001` refuses a real
public-source structural failure when the stronger official-source explanation
is newly introduced or changed connection design, hanger-rod arrangement,
calculation and shop-drawing review, construction-detail communication, and
load-capacity failure rather than source-backed reuse of an old assumption
under changed conditions.

It does not create an accepted topology schema, structural-engineering model,
building-code conclusion, professional-liability conclusion, construction
review standard, legal conclusion, CAI doctrine, intervention plan, or owner
routing instruction.

No confidential material, human subjects, field work, account access, external
contact, posting, spending, publication outside normal GitHub versioning, or
deployment was used.

## Absorber Question

The provisional candidate should refuse this case as positive evidence if its
old-assumption gate is doing real work.

The NBS/NIST source set centers insufficient load capacity of the box
beam-hanger rod connections, inadequacy of the original connection design, and
a construction-phase hanger-rod arrangement change that substantially increased
the load transferred through fourth-floor walkway connections. Those facts
create real review, communication, and calculation lessons. They are still not
enough for this repository to classify the case as a review-chain assumption-
reuse record under `SF-SCHEMA-CANDIDATE-0001`.

The case is useful because it creates tempting surface fit: an approved design
context changed during construction, review and approval paths are visible,
affected-party standing is explicit through public harm, and official source
material names correction-relevant engineering and review failures. Positive
fit still requires a source-backed old assumption reused under changed
conditions, visible validation burden, delegation or review fragmentation
material to that contradiction, absorber control, and an owner-visible
correction route. Here, the narrower new-assumption, design-change,
connection-capacity, calculation-review, shop-drawing-review, and construction-
communication account absorbs the candidate.

## Verdict

`SF-SCHEMA-CANDIDATE-0001` refuses this case as positive evidence. That refusal
is useful after the resource-adequacy absorber because it shows that a public,
multi-actor disaster with a changed design context, review visibility,
affected-party standing, and correction-route visibility does not qualify when
the strongest official-source explanation is newly introduced or revised design
detail, connection capacity, hanger-rod arrangement, calculation review,
shop-drawing review, and construction communication rather than the candidate's
source-backed old-assumption reuse residue.

The candidate remains provisional and local to review-chain failures involving
source-backed old-assumption reuse under changed conditions, visible validation
burden, affected standing, absorber control, and correction-route discipline.
This negative absorber does not justify schema acceptance.

## Candidate Revision Pressure

Keep the candidate field set as-is, but add one refusal note to the candidate:
when public official sources center newly introduced design, configuration,
shop-drawing, construction-detail, calculation-review, or change-control
assumptions rather than source-backed reuse of an old assumption under changed
conditions, the case should be recorded as an absorber or separate topology
question, not positive evidence for this candidate.

This note must remain source-gated. It does not authorize this repository to
make structural-engineering, building-code, construction-management,
professional-liability, legal, regulatory, enforcement, inspection, design-
